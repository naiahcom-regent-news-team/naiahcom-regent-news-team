About Our News Team

Dedicated to truth, excellence, and student voices

Our Mission

At Naiahcom High School Regent, our dedicated student journalists work hard to bring you the latest news from around our school and community. From local events to international stories, our team covers it all with professionalism and enthusiasm.

We believe in the power of student voices and strive to deliver accurate, engaging, and relevant content to our school community, guided by our school motto: Veritas Omnia Vincit (Truth Conquers All).

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of journalistic ethics and truthfulness.

Voice

We amplify student perspectives and celebrate diverse experiences.

Innovation

We embrace new storytelling methods and digital platforms.

Community

We serve and connect with our school community through impactful reporting.
